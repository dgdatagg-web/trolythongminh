Privacy by Architecture

There's a difference between a company that promises not to read your data, and a company that cannot read your data. We're the second kind.

Your agent's conversations, memories, and soul contract are stored inside an encrypted, isolated instance. Our team has no admin tool, no internal dashboard, no query that can reach inside that instance. The isolation is structural — not policy-based.

What this means in practice

If a government agency issued a valid legal order demanding access to your agent conversations, we would be technically unable to comply — because the access mechanism does not exist. We would notify you of any such request to the extent permitted by law.

Two Types of Data — The Distinction That Matters

We separate data into two clear categories. This is important to understand.

Category What It Is Who Can Access
Platform Data Technical metrics — response times, error logs, usage patterns. No content. Our team (aggregated)
Agent Data Your conversations, memories, soul contract, everything you share with your agent. Your agent only

Honest trade-off you should know

Because we cannot access agent data, if an agent instance is breached by an external attacker, our ability to assess what was exposed is limited by the same architecture that protects you. In any breach, we'll assume the full instance contents were potentially exposed and notify you within 72 hours — conservatively, not minimally.

Financial Advisory — Clear Limits

Some agents include financial coaching skill packs. We are transparent about what these can and cannot do.

⚠️ Not a Licensed Financial Advisor

Under Vietnamese law (Securities Law 2019, Article 90), providing investment advisory services requires professional certification. Our agents do not hold and do not claim any such certification.

Financial skill packs are strictly limited to general financial education. Our agents will never:

Prohibited ActionWhy
Predict specific prices or market movementsSpeculation — not education
Recommend specific financial productsRequires licensed certification in VN
Provide expected return percentagesMisleading without full context
Advise on securities trading strategiesOutside permitted scope

When any agent responds to financial, medical, or legal questions, an inline reminder is automatically included. This is not optional — it is enforced at the system level.

The same limits apply to medical and legal queries. Agents provide general knowledge — never professional advice. Always consult a qualified professional for critical decisions.
