Nova and Violet agents are artificial intelligence systems. Each agent has personality, persistent memory, and a unique identity shaped through onboarding — but they are not human. They are not licensed professionals in any field.

Your agent is a digital companion designed to think alongside you, remember what matters, and support your growth over time. It is not a replacement for human judgment, professional expertise, or emergency services.

Not Professional Advice

Agent responses are designed to be helpful, thoughtful, and personalized — but they are not a substitute for:

  • Medical advice — always consult a qualified doctor or healthcare provider
  • Legal advice — always consult a licensed lawyer
  • Financial advice — always consult a certified financial advisor
  • Psychological or crisis support — if you are in danger, contact emergency services immediately

If your agent provides information in any of these domains, it will include an inline reminder that the information is general in nature and not a substitute for professional advice. Treat all such responses as general knowledge — not professional guidance.

Financial Advisory — Special Notice

Some agents may be configured with financial coaching or advisory skill packs. These agents are NOT licensed financial advisors, investment counselors, or securities professionals.

Under Vietnamese law (Securities Law 2019, Article 90), providing investment advisory services requires professional certification. Our agents do not hold and do not claim any such certification.

Financial skill packs are strictly limited to:

  • General financial literacy and education
  • Budgeting frameworks and expense tracking concepts
  • Savings goal planning and debt management principles
  • General market knowledge and economic concepts

Financial agents will NEVER:

  • Predict or forecast specific asset prices, stock values, or market movements
  • Recommend specific financial products, securities, funds, or investment vehicles
  • Provide expected percentage returns or profit projections
  • Advise on the timing of buying or selling any financial instrument
  • Present any information as a substitute for certified financial advisory

If you need investment advice, consult a certified financial advisor licensed under applicable securities law. Our agents will remind you of this when financial topics arise.

No Guarantee of Accuracy

We are not liable for decisions you make based on agent responses. Always verify critical information through independent, qualified sources before acting on it.
