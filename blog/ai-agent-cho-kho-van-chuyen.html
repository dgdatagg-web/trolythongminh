AI Agent Cho Kho Van Chuyen: Tu Dong Theo Doi Don Hang, Thong Bao, Xu Ly Khieu Nai

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 7 phut doc

📌 Key Takeaways

  • 60–80% cuoc goi va tin nhan hotline cua cong ty van chuyen la cau hoi "hang cua toi dau roi?" — cau hoi co the tra loi tu dong 100%.
  • AI agent tu dong cap nhat trang thai don hang, gui thong bao khi hang den kho — giam tai hotline 50–70% va tang suc hanh vi khach hang.
  • 4 use case core: theo doi don hang tu dong, thong bao trang thai chu dong, xu ly FAQ van chuyen, va bao cao dinh ky cho khach B2B.
  • Nganh logistics Viet Nam xu ly hon 3 ty don hang moi nam (EVBN, 2024) — volume nay buoc cac cong ty phai tu dong hoa CSKH de ton tai.
  • ROI cao nhat: moi nhan vien CSKH xu ly 80–100 cuoc goi/ngay. AI agent xu ly hang ngan, khong met moi, khong sai ngon tren cac cau hoi tieu chuan.

Mot cong ty van chuyen vua xu ly 500 don hang/ngay nhan tung ay cuoc goi. "Hang toi den chua?", "Don [ma kien] dau roi?", "Tai sao chua nhan duoc?" — hotline doi khi busy ca buoi sang chi de tra loi nhung cau hoi ma he thong da co du lieu. Day khong phai van de cua con nguoi — day la van de cua quy trinh. Va AI agent giai quyet dung quy trinh do.

"65% yeu cau hotline cua cong ty van chuyen la 'tra loi thong tin co san'. AI agent xu ly toan bo nhung cau hoi do, de hotline tap trung vao nhung gi thuc su can con nguoi."

Vi sao kho van chuyen dac biet phu hop voi AI agent?

Logistics va kho van chuyen co 3 dac diem lam cho AI agent hieu qua cao nhat:

Dac diem 1 — Volume cao

Yeu cau theo doi hang la volume cao nhat, lap nhat

"Hang toi dau roi?" — cau hoi nay co the duoc hoi hang tram lan moi ngay, moi lan tu mot khach khac nhau ve mot don hang khac nhau, nhung cau tra loi la cung mot quy trinh: tra cuu ma kien trong he thong, doc trang thai, bao cho khach. AI xu ly nhanh hon, chinh xac hon, khong bao gio de ngoai gio.

Dac diem 2 — Data co cau truc

Du lieu van chuyen la co cau truc, de doc may

Ma kien, ngay gui, trang thai hien tai, lich su di chuyen, thoi gian du kien giao — tat ca la du lieu so co the truy van tu dong. Khac voi cac nganh can cam nhan con nguoi (tu van, xu ly cam xuc), theo doi van chuyen la tac vu don gian ma AI lam tot hon con nguoi vi khong bao gio nham ma kien.

Dac diem 3 — Pro-active value

Thong bao chu dong lam khach hai long hon

Khong ai thich phai goi hotline de hoi "hang cua toi dau roi?". Khi AI agent tu dong gui thong bao "hang cua ban da den kho TP.HCM, du kien giao ngay mai" — khach khong can hoi, lo lang giam, hanh vi khach hang tang. Day la chuyen tu reactive sang proactive — va do la diem khac biet that su cua dich vu logistics chat luong cao.

4 use case AI agent thuc te cho kho van chuyen

Use case 1 — Core

Tra loi "hang toi dau roi?" tu dong 24/7

Flow thuc te: Khach nhan tin "hang cua toi dau?" → AI hoi ma kien hoac so dien thoai nguoi gui → tra cuu he thong → bao trang thai: "Kien [ma] cua ban dang o kho Ha Noi, du kien giao ngay [DATE]. Xem chi tiet tai: [link]" → neu co delay: "Kien bi cham hon ke hoach do [ly do]. Du kien giao la [DATE moi]." Tac dong: Giam 50–70% cuoc goi hotline de hoi trang thai.

Use case 2 — Pro-active

Thong bao trang thai chu dong

Flow thuc te: Khi hang den kho trung chuyen: tu dong gui Zalo/SMS: "Kien [ma] da den kho [ten kho] luc [gio]. Du kien giao den ban trong [X] ngay lam viec." Khi giao khong thanh cong: "Chung toi da thu giao kien [ma] nhung khong co nguoi nhan. Vui long xac nhan lich giao lai hoac den kho tu lay." Tac dong: Tang ty le giao thanh cong lan dau 10–20%, giam chi phi giao lai.

Use case 3 — B2B reporting

Bao cao dinh ky va portal tu dong cho khach B2B

Flow thuc te: Moi thu hai, AI tu dong gui bao cao hang tuan cho cac cong ty doi tac: so kien gui, so kien da giao, ty le giao dung han, cac kien con ton dong va ly do. Khach B2B co the hoi bat ky luc nao: "Thang nay minh co bao nhieu kien tri hoan?" → AI tra loi ngay voi du lieu thuc. Tac dong: Tang su hai long khach hang doanh nghiep, giu hop dong dai han.

Use case 4 — Onboarding

Huong dan khach moi gui hang va bao gia

Flow thuc te: Khach moi hoi "gui hang tu Ha Noi vao Sai Gon het bao nhieu, bao lau?" → AI hoi kich thuoc, can nang → tinh phi va thoi gian du kien theo bang gia co san → huong dan dong goi chuan → cung cap link dang ky tai khoan khach hang. Khong can sales phai goi lai tung khach moi. Tac dong: Rut ngan thoi gian tu hoi gia den gui hang dau tien tu 2–3 ngay xuong vai gio.

"Mot cong ty van chuyen 10 nhan vien CSKH xu ly 800 cuoc goi/ngay. 65% la hoi trang thai don hang. AI agent xu ly toan bo 65% do = giai phong 6 nhan vien de xu ly khieu nai phuc tap va cham soc khach B2B — nhung viec co gia tri that su." — NOVA, tu trien khai thuc te.

Gioi han can biet: AI agent khong thay the dispatcher va xu ly su co

⚠️ Nhung gi AI agent KHONG lam duoc cho kho van chuyen:
• Dieu phoi xe tu dong: Phan cong xe, tuyen duong, uu tien giao hang — can dispatcher voi kinh nghiem va hieu biet dia ly thuc te.
• Xu ly hang hu hong / mat mat: Khieu nai boi thuong, danh gia thiet hai, dam phan boi thuong — can nghiep vu va thiem quyen cua nhan vien co tham quyen.
• Xu ly hai quan va thu tuc phap ly: Giay to nhap khau, thue hai quan, kiem tra chuyen — can chuyen vien co chung chi.
• Xac nhan don hang dac biet: Hang nguy hiem, hang dong lanh, hang gia tri cao — can kiem tra thu cong va ky xac nhan.

Chi phi va ROI thuc te

💡 Vi du tinh toan ROI cho cong ty van chuyen 300 don hang/ngay:
• Hotline hien tai: 3 nhan vien CSKH, luong trung binh 8 trieu/nguoi = 24 trieu/thang
• 65% cuoc goi la trang thai don hang = AI agent thay the duoc
• Tiet kiem: khong can tuyen them nhan vien khi volume tang 2–3x, tinh tiet kiem chi phi tang truong: 15–20 trieu/thang
• Giao hang thanh cong lan dau tang 15% (nho thong bao proactive) = giam chi phi giao lai: trung binh 30k/kien giao lai × 45 kien = 1.35 trieu/ngay = 40 trieu/thang
• Chi phi AI agent: 5 trieu setup + 600k/thang = 12.2 trieu/nam
• Net ROI thang dau: +55 trieu/thang

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

AI agent co tich hop duoc voi phan mem quan ly kho (WMS) hien tai khong?
Tuy thuoc phan mem. Cac WMS co API co the tich hop truc tiep — AI agent doc trang thai don hang va tra loi theo thoi gian thuc. Voi phan mem khong co API, co the dung Google Sheets lam trung gian: he thong cap nhat Sheets, AI agent doc tu Sheets. Setup don gian hon nhung co the delay 5–15 phut.
Neu hang bi mat hoac hong, AI agent xu ly the nao?
Day la truong hop can nguoi that. AI agent tiep nhan khieu nai ban dau, ghi nhan ma kien, xac nhan cho khach: "Chung toi da ghi nhan khieu nai. Team xu ly se lien he trong vong 4 gio lam viec." Sau do chuyen ngay cho team. Khong de AI tu dong chap nhan boi thuong hay quyet dinh ket qua khieu nai.
AI agent co xu ly duoc nhieu loai khach B2B va B2C cung luc khong?
Co — AI agent co the cau hinh voi nhieu flow rieng: khach B2B (hop dong, bao cao dinh ky, hoa don) va khach B2C (ca nhan gui le, theo doi nhanh). Key la nhan dien loai khach tu dau de route dung flow.
Cong ty van chuyen nho 5–10 xe co can AI agent khong?
Co. Cong ty nho bi ton nhieu thoi gian hon cho cac cau hoi lap vi khong co doi ngu CSKH rieng. Chu cong ty thuong phai tu tra loi dien thoai, phan tan tap trung khi dang dieu phoi xe. AI agent tra loi 80% cau hoi thuong gap, chu cong ty chi xu ly nhung gi thuc su can su can thiep.
Lam the nao de AI agent gui bao cao tong ket cho khach hang B2B?
AI agent co the lap lich tu dong tao va gui bao cao dinh ky (hang tuan hoac hang thang): so kien da gui, so kien da nhan, ty le dung han, cac kien co su co. Format co the la bang tom tat qua Zalo/email, hoac link den bao cao chi tiet. Khach B2B danh gia cao tinh minh bach nay — giup giu hop dong dai han.
