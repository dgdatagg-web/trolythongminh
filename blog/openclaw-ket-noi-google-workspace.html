Ket Noi OpenClaw Voi Google Workspace: Sheets, Docs, Gmail, Drive, Calendar

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 7 phut doc
💬 Tra Loi Nhanh

OpenClaw co the ket noi voi Google Sheets, Docs, Gmail, Drive va Calendar thong qua Google Workspace MCP server hoac Skills tuy chinh. Khi ket noi xong, bot Telegram cua anh/chi co the doc du lieu tu Sheets, gui email qua Gmail, tao lich trong Calendar, va truy cap file tren Drive. Tuy nhien, qua trinh setup can OAuth2 credentials tu Google Cloud Console — day la buoc non-technical users hay bi mac ket nhat, thuong mat 1–3 ngay neu chua quen voi Google Cloud. Tong chi phi phan mem: mien phi. Chi phi thoi gian: day la van de chinh.

📋 Diem Chinh Can Nho

  • OpenClaw + Google Workspace ket noi duoc — thong qua MCP server va OAuth2
  • Can tao project tren Google Cloud Console va bat tung API (Sheets, Gmail, Drive...)
  • Khi xong: bot co the doc/ghi Sheets, gui Gmail, quan ly lich tu cuoc hoi thoai Telegram
  • Day la tinh nang "after setup" — pho bien nhat sau khi da co OpenClaw chay on dinh
  • Goi 2M/thang bao gom setup tich hop nay + ho tro khi co van de phat sinh

Tai sao 2+ nguoi dung hoi ve Google Workspace trong cung 1 tuan?

Trong tuan 9–10/03/2026, it nhat 2 nguoi dung thuc te da hoi minh cung 1 chu de: lam the nao de OpenClaw ket noi voi Google Workspace. Mot nguoi muon bot doc doanh thu tu Google Sheets roi tong hop bao cao hang ngay. Mot nguoi khac muon bot gui email xac nhan don hang qua Gmail tu dong khi co tin nhan Telegram.

Noi dau giong nhau: "Toi biet OpenClaw co the lam duoc, nhung toi khong biet bat dau tu dau."

Day la noi dau co ly. Ket noi OpenClaw voi Google Workspace khong phai bam mot nut la xong — no doi hoi hieu biet ve OAuth2, Google Cloud Console, va cach cau hinh MCP server. Voi nguoi non-tech, day la mot leap kha lon.

Ket noi hoat dong nhu the nao — giai thich don gian

Kien truc ket noi gom 3 lop:

Lop La gi? Anh/chi can lam gi?
OpenClaw AI gateway dang chay cua anh/chi Da co san (neu da setup)
Google Workspace MCP Cau noi giua OpenClaw va cac dich vu Google Cau hinh Skills hoac MCP server entry
Google Cloud OAuth2 Xac thuc — cho phep bot truy cap tai khoan Google Tao credentials tren Google Cloud Console

Lop kho nhat voi nguoi non-tech la Google Cloud OAuth2. Anh/chi can:

  1. Tao project tren Google Cloud Console
  2. Bat tung API can dung: Google Sheets API, Gmail API, Google Drive API, Google Calendar API
  3. Cau hinh OAuth2 Consent Screen (internal hoac external)
  4. Tao OAuth2 Client ID va Client Secret
  5. Luu credentials vao file JSON, dua vao cau hinh OpenClaw

Moi buoc nay co nut nhap rieng. Sai 1 buoc — ket noi khong hoat dong, va error message cua Google thuong kha kho hieu voi nguoi chua quen.

⚠️ Diem hay bi mac ket nhat: Nhieu nguoi tao OAuth2 credentials dung nhung quen khong bat API tuong ung (vi du: bat Google Drive API nhung quen bat Google Sheets API). Ket qua: mot so tinh nang hoat dong, mot so thi khong — rat kho debug.

OpenClaw co the lam gi sau khi ket noi Google Workspace?

Day la phan thu vi. Khi da ket noi xong, bot Telegram cua anh/chi co the:

Dich vu Google Bot co the lam gi? Vi du thuc te
Google Sheets Doc du lieu, them row, filter, tong hop "Tuan nay ban duoc bao nhieu hang?" → bot tra loi tu Sheets thuc
Gmail Gui email, doc inbox, tim email theo keyword "Gui email xac nhan don hang 123 cho khach" → bot gui tu Gmail cua anh/chi
Google Drive Liet ke file, doc noi dung, tao folder "File bao gia thang truoc dau?" → bot tim trong Drive va tra loi
Google Docs Doc noi dung, them text, tao doc moi "Tom tat proposal thang nay cho minh" → bot doc va tong hop
Google Calendar Xem lich, tao su kien, nhac lich "Tuan nay co cuoc hop nao?" → bot tra loi tu Calendar thuc cua anh/chi

Noi gon: bot Telegram bien thanh tro ly thuc su biet business cua anh/chi — khong phai bot tra loi chung chung, ma bot biet du lieu thuc, lich thuc, email thuc cua doanh nghiep.

Tai sao kho tu lam — va it nguoi vuot qua buoc nay

Khi minh noi "kho," day khong phai de doa. Day la nhung gi minh quan sat tu thuc te:

1. Google Cloud Console khong friendly voi nguoi moi. Interface nhieu option, nhieu menu, nhieu khai niem ky thuat (API, credentials, scopes, service account...). Nguoi chua quen co the mat 1–2 gio chi de tim dung chon nao can bam.

2. OAuth2 flow can test va debug. Sau khi co credentials, can test flow xac thuc trong moi truong OpenClaw. Neu sai scope — xac thuc duoc nhung khong lam duoc gi. Neu sai redirect URI — xac thuc fail hoan toan.

3. MCP server config voi OpenClaw can matching chinh xac. Skills config phai match dung voi credentials Google, dung voi ten field trong JSON. Mot chu sai — ca he thong khong ket noi.

Nhung khi da setup xong — no chay on dinh, khong can phai lam lai. Day la ly do "setup 1 lan, dung lau dai" — nhung "1 lan" do co the la mot tuan voi nguoi non-tech.

Day chinh xac la nhu cau ma goi 2M/thang giai quyet

Nhieu nguoi nghi setup OpenClaw la xong. Thuc ra, ket noi Google Workspace la buoc tiep theo sau setup — va no thuong kho hon setup ban dau voi nguoi khong co ky thuat.

Goi Basic 2M/thang cua NOVA bao gom:

  • Setup OpenClaw hoan chinh tren VPS
  • Ket noi Google Workspace theo dich vu anh/chi can (Sheets, Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Docs)
  • Test va verify tung tinh nang truoc khi ban giao
  • Ho tro khi co van de phat sinh sau do
  • Update khi OpenClaw ra phien ban moi

Anh/chi khong can biet Google Cloud Console la gi. Khong can biet OAuth2. Khong can debug loi ket noi. Minh lam het — anh/chi chi can nhan bot va dung.

Muon biet them ve qua trinh setup tong the? Xem bai OpenClaw cai dat nhu the nao? de co buc tranh day du hon.

Sau khi da ket noi Google Workspace, buoc tiep theo pho bien la toi uu hoa bot de no hoat dong dung y muon. Xem bai Toi uu OpenClaw sau khi setup de hieu buoc nay can gi.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

OpenClaw co the ket noi voi Google Sheets khong?
Co. OpenClaw ket noi Google Sheets thong qua Google Sheets API va OAuth2. Sau khi ket noi, bot co the doc du lieu, them row moi, filter theo dieu kien — tat ca tu cuoc hoi thoai Telegram. Vi du: "bao cao doanh thu tuan nay" va bot tu dong lay tu Sheets va tra loi.
Can biet lap trinh de ket noi OpenClaw voi Google Workspace khong?
Khong can viet code, nhung can hieu cach cau hinh OAuth2 tren Google Cloud Console va chinh sua file JSON config cua OpenClaw. Day la buoc trung cap — khong phai lap trinh, nhung cung khong phai click-and-done.
Mat bao lau de setup OpenClaw + Google Workspace?
Nguoi co ky thuat: 2–4 gio cho toan bo OAuth2 + config + test. Nguoi khong co ky thuat: co the mat 1–3 ngay vi chua quen Google Cloud Console va OAuth flow. Khi xong thi on dinh — khong can setup lai.
OpenClaw co the gui email qua Gmail cua minh khong?
Co. Sau khi cau hinh Gmail API + OAuth2, bot co the gui email tu tai khoan Gmail cua anh/chi — viet noi dung tu nhien bang tieng Viet, them attachment, gui den nhieu nguoi nhan. Tat ca tu lenh Telegram don gian.
Co dich vu nao setup OpenClaw + Google Workspace thay minh khong?
Co. NOVA cung cap dich vu setup tich hop day du — OpenClaw + Google Workspace (Sheets, Docs, Gmail, Drive, Calendar) — hoan chinh trong 48 gio. Anh/chi dung ngay qua Telegram ma khong can biet OAuth hay Google Cloud Console. Dat lich tu van mien phi.
