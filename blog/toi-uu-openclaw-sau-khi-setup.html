Toi Uu OpenClaw Sau Khi Setup: Model, Memory, SOUL.md — Bot Chay Dung Y Muon

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 8 phut doc
💬 Tra Loi Nhanh

Setup xong roi nhung bot chay chua dung y la noi dau pho bien nhat sau khi co OpenClaw. Co 3 nguyen nhan chinh: (1) SOUL.md van la mac dinh — bot khong biet gi ve business cua anh/chi; (2) Model chua duoc chon theo tung task — moi cuoc hoi thoai deu dung Sonnet du phan lon chi can model nhe hon; (3) Memory chua cau hinh — bot quen moi context sau moi cuoc hoi thoai. Ba viec nay khong phai "one-time fix" ma la recurring optimization khi business thay doi.

📋 Diem Chinh Can Nho

  • OpenClaw mac dinh la generic — can tuy chinh moi hoat dong dung cho business cua anh/chi
  • SOUL.md quyet dinh 70% chat luong trai nghiem — day la file quan trong nhat can chinh
  • Chon model sai = tra phi cao hon can thiet HOAC bot chay cham va kem hon muc co the
  • Memory cau hinh sai = bot quen khach hang sau moi cuoc hoi thoai — trai nghiem roi
  • Goi 2M/thang cua NOVA bao gom toi uu lien tuc — khong chi setup 1 lan

Tai sao "setup xong" khong co nghia la "bot da tot"

Day la phan khong ai noi thang: khi anh/chi setup xong OpenClaw va bot da tra loi duoc, day moi chi la foundation — khong phai ket qua cuoi.

OpenClaw mac dinh la mot AI gateway thuan tuy. No biet tat ca moi thu theo nghia chung, nhung no khong biet:

  • Business cua anh/chi ban gi, phuc vu ai, gia tri cot loi la gi
  • Cach noi chuyen phu hop voi khach hang cua anh/chi
  • Nhung gi bot duoc lam va khong duoc lam
  • Lich su cua khach hang da tuong tac truoc do

Ket qua: bot tra loi dung nhung cam giac "chung chung" — nhu ChatGPT thuan, khong phai nhu mot nhan vien biet business. Khach hang nhan ra su khac biet nay ngay.

⚠️ Thuc te hay gap: Nhieu nguoi setup xong, cho ban be thu va nghe feedback "ok nhung chung qua," roi bo qua buoc toi uu vi nghi "the nay la du roi." Bot chung chung khong tao ra gia tri kinh doanh — chi co bot biet business moi thay doi duoc trai nghiem khach hang.

SOUL.md — file quan trong nhat anh/chi chua chinh

SOUL.md la file dinh nghia tinh cach va boi canh cua bot. No tra loi cac cau hoi:

  • Bot ten gi? Phuong cach noi chuyen nhu the nao?
  • Bot phuc vu ai? Muc dich chinh la gi?
  • Nhung nguyen tac nao bot phai tuan theo?
  • Khi gap cau hoi kho, bot xu ly nhu the nao?
  • Gioi han cua bot la gi — dieu gi se duoc chuyen cho nguoi that?

Mac dinh SOUL.md la template chung. Khi chua chinh, bot se:

  • Tra loi theo phong cach chung chung cua AI
  • Khong phan biet minh dang phuc vu cua hang nao
  • Co the tra loi ve nhung chu de khong lien quan den business
  • Tone noi chuyen khong match voi brand cua anh/chi

Mot SOUL.md tot cho bot CSKH F&B se hoan toan khac voi SOUL.md cho bot tro ly noi bo cong ty. Viec chinh SOUL.md dung — va chau chuot theo phan hoi thuc te — la cong viec khong co diem ket thuc, vi business thay doi theo thoi gian.

Chon model: Sonnet, Opus, hay mix?

Day la quyet dinh anh huong ca den chat luong lan chi phi hang thang. OpenClaw cho phep cau hinh model khac nhau cho tung loai request.

Model Tot nhat cho Chi phi tuong doi Khi nao tranh dung
Claude Haiku Cau hoi ngan, FAQ don gian, trigger nhanh Thap nhat (~1x) Tasks can reasoning sau hoac viet content dai
Claude Sonnet CSKH, tro ly hang ngay, phan tich trung binh Trung binh (~5x Haiku) Cau hoi ngan co the dung Haiku; tasks phuc tap nen dung Opus
Claude Opus Phan tich phuc tap, viet content chieu sau, chien luoc Cao nhat (~30x Haiku) CSKH thuong nhat, cau hoi FAQ — qua dat cho muc do can thiet
GPT-4o Tasks can xu ly hinh anh + text ket hop Trung binh Thuong ngon task text-only so voi Claude Sonnet

Cau hinh pho bien nhat cho SME VN: Sonnet cho 80% requests + Opus chi cho 20% tasks phuc tap. Neu dang dung 100% Opus — anh/chi dang tra phi gap 3–5 lan can thiet.

Nguoc lai, neu dang dung 100% Haiku de tiet kiem — chat luong tra loi co the khong du tot cho CSKH, tao an tuong xau voi khach.

Memory — de bot nho khach hang

Memory la tinh nang chia "bot co nho khong" thanh 2 loai:

Conversation memory (ngan han): Bot nho context trong cung mot cuoc hoi thoai. Mac dinh da co — nhung window ngan. Bot se quen neu cuoc hoi thoai qua dai hoac sau khi restart.

Long-term memory (dai han): Bot nho thong tin khach hang qua nhieu ngay, nhieu cuoc hoi thoai. Phan nay can cau hinh rieng — luu vao file hoac database — va quyet dinh "bot co biet khach la ai" hay khong.

Vi du thuc te cho thay su khac biet:

  • Chua co long-term memory: Khach nhan Telegram lan 2, ho lai phai tu gioi thieu: "Minh la Hai, order do tuyet hom qua..." — trai nghiem roi.
  • Co long-term memory: Bot tu dong nhan ra: "Chao chi Hai! Don hang cua chi hom qua da giao chua? Chi co can ho tro gi them khong?" — trai nghiem nhu nhan vien that.

Cau hinh memory dung — biet luu gi, luu den bao gio, biet xoa gi — la cong viec trung cap, can hieu ro kien truc OpenClaw moi lam dung.

Skills va AGENTS.md — mo rong kha nang bot

Sau khi da toi uu SOUL.md, model va memory, buoc tiep theo la mo rong kha nang bot qua Skills. OpenClaw co he thong Skills cho phep bot:

  • Doc va ghi file (Excel, Google Sheets, tai lieu)
  • Truy cap internet, search thong tin moi nhat
  • Ket noi API ben ngoai (CRM, ecommerce platform, cong cu noi bo)
  • Thuc hien nhieu cuoc hoi thoai song song

Moi Skill la mot module rieng — co the bat/tat theo nhu cau. Nhung cau hinh va test tung Skill can thoi gian va hieu biet ky thuat.

Muon ket noi them voi Google Workspace (Sheets, Docs, Gmail)? Xem bai Ket noi OpenClaw voi Google Workspace de hieu buoc tiep theo.

Tai sao day la cong viec recurring, khong phai one-time

Mot cau hoi minh hay nghe: "Setup xong roi minh can phai chieu chuot OpenClaw bao nhieu?"

Cau tra loi phu thuoc vao business cua anh/chi. Nhung nhin chung — bot can duoc cap nhat khi:

  • Business ra san pham/dich vu moi — bot can biet
  • Co phan hoi "bot tra loi sai" hoac "bot chua hieu y" tu khach hang — can chinh SOUL.md
  • Model moi ra mat va tot hon model hien tai — co the chuyen sang de giam chi phi hoac tang chat luong
  • Co van de ky thuat: bot cham, loi, hoac khong ket noi duoc
  • Muon them tinh nang moi (ket noi them ung dung, them nguon data...)

Day la ly do goi Basic 2M/thang ton tai — khong phai de setup 1 lan roi thoi. Ma la de co nguoi dong hanh khi business thay doi, khi co van de, khi muon mo rong.

Neu anh/chi chua co OpenClaw chay on dinh, doc bai OpenClaw cai dat nhu the nao truoc — buoc nay phai xong truoc khi toi uu.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Tai sao OpenClaw da setup xong nhung bot van tra loi chung chung, khong dung y?
Vi cau hinh mac dinh cua OpenClaw la generic — khong biet gi ve business cua anh/chi. Can chinh SOUL.md theo dung business, chon model phu hop, va cau hinh memory de bot nho context. Day la buoc "after setup" ma 80% nguoi dung bo qua — va la su khac biet giua bot "ok" va bot "that su hieu business."
Claude Sonnet hay Claude Opus tot hon cho business nho?
Sonnet la diem can bang tot nhat cho phan lon business nho: nhanh, re hon Opus khoang 6x, du thong minh cho CSKH, tong hop va tra loi chat. Chi dung Opus khi can reasoning sau (phan tich phuc tap, chien luoc). Mix model theo task la cach toi uu nhat — Sonnet 80%, Opus 20%.
SOUL.md la gi va can chinh nhu the nao?
SOUL.md la file dinh nghia "tinh cach" cua bot — ten, phong cach noi chuyen, nguyen tac, gioi han. Mac dinh la generic. Can tuy chinh theo tung business: neu la CSKH F&B thi khac bot tro ly ke toan, thi khac bot khach hang. Chinh dung SOUL.md la buoc quyet dinh chat luong trai nghiem nhieu nhat.
OpenClaw co memory — bot co nho cac cuoc hoi thoai truoc khong?
Co. OpenClaw co he thong memory co the cau hinh — conversation memory (ngan han, mac dinh co) va long-term memory (nho qua nhieu cuoc hoi thoai, can cau hinh rieng). Long-term memory la phan quyet dinh "bot co biet khach la ai" hay khong — anh huong lon den trai nghiem khach hang.
Co ai giup toi uu OpenClaw sau khi setup khong?
Co. NOVA cung cap dich vu optimize OpenClaw sau setup — chinh SOUL.md theo business, cau hinh model phu hop, setup memory va Skills. Goi 2M/thang bao gom ca viec nay lien tuc theo nhu cau thay doi cua business. Dat lich tu van mien phi de bat dau.
