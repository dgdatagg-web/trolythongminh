AI Agent Cho To Chuc Su Kien: Tu Dong Dang Ky, Nhac Nhach, CSKH Truoc Va Sau Event

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 7 phut doc

📌 Key Takeaways

  • Team to chuc su kien mat 60–80% thoi gian truoc event cho cac cong viec lap: tra loi FAQ dang ky, xu ly thay doi thong tin, gui xac nhan va nhac nhach tham du vien.
  • AI agent tu dong hoa toan bo quy trinh truoc — trong — sau su kien: dang ky, xac nhan, nhac nhach, check-in, thu thap feedback.
  • Ty le no-show tai su kien Viet Nam trung binh 20–35% (nguoi dang ky nhung khong den). AI agent nhac truoc 24h va 2h giam no-show xuong 10–15%.
  • 4 use case core: xu ly dang ky va FAQ 24/7, nhac nhach truoc su kien, ho tro check-in, va thu thap feedback sau event tu dong.
  • Voi su kien 200+ nguoi tham du, AI agent tiet kiem 15–20 gio cong viec thu cong cua team moi lan to chuc.

Ban to chuc su kien 300 nguoi. Chay quang cao, khach dang ky, roi 3 ngay truoc su kien inbox Zalo bang: "Cho hoi su kien o dau?", "Ve cua minh co hieu luc chua?", "Co the doi lich khong?", "Cho minh hoi lich trinh ngay do the nao?" — hang tram tin nhan cung mot loai, team CSKH chay khong kip. AI agent co the xu ly 90% so do tu dong, 24/7, trong khi team tap trung vao nhung viec thuc su can con nguoi.

"Su kien 300 nguoi, 35% no-show = 105 ghe trong. Chi can AI agent nhac truoc 24h giam no-show xuong 15%, ban giu them 60 nguoi tham du — trai nghiem tot hon, doanh thu cao hon."

Vi sao to chuc su kien dac biet can AI agent?

Nganh su kien co 3 dac diem tao ra cong viec lap cuc lon ma AI agent co the giai quyet:

Dac diem 1 — Volume

Tin nhan dang ky & FAQ tang dot bien truoc event

Khi mo dang ky: hang tram nguoi hoi cung mot luc. Khi gan den ngay su kien: lai mot dot nua. Team CSKH 2–3 nguoi khong the xu ly kip luong tin nhan nay. Ket qua: khach hoi khong co ai tra loi, bo qua, mat dang ky — hoac team lam viec ngoai gio khong cong. AI agent xu ly toan bo hang dot thu nhat cua cac cau hoi tieu chuan.

Dac diem 2 — No-show

No-show gay lang phi dang ke

Su kien co phi: no-show la mat doanh thu co the thu. Su kien mien phi: no-show lam dai sanh trong, den phan, huong nghich voi hinh anh. No-show nganh su kien VN khoang 20–35% — mot phan do khach quen, mot phan do khach dang ky nhieu su kien cung luc roi khong co ai nhac. Nhac truoc 24h va 2h giam ty le nay ro ret.

Dac diem 3 — Relationship

Sau su kien la co hoi xay dung quan he

Nguoi tham du su kien la khach hang tiem nang nhiet tinh nhat. Nhung 80% cong ty to chuc su kien khong co he thong follow-up sau event. AI agent tu dong gui loi cam on, link tai lieu, link feedback form, va cac thong tin su kien tiep theo — bien nguoi tham du thanh khach hang trung thanh.

4 use case AI agent thuc te cho to chuc su kien

Use case 1 — Dang ky

Xu ly dang ky va FAQ 24/7

Flow thuc te: Khach nhan tin "cho hoi su kien [ten] con cho dang ky khong?" → AI kiem tra so luong ve con lai → giai thich cac loai ve va gia → huong dan dang ky → sau khi dang ky xong: gui email/Zalo xac nhan voi thong tin su kien, QR check-in, va lich trinh. Khong can le tan truc man 24/7 trong giai doan mo ban ve. Tac dong: Tang ty le dang ky 15–25% tu cai thien thoi gian phan hoi.

Use case 2 — Impact cao nhat

Nhac nhach truoc su kien: -24h va -2h

Flow thuc te: Tu dong gui truoc 24 gio: "Ngay mai la [ten su kien]! Nho co mat tai [dia diem] luc [gio]. Check-in bang QR code nay: [QR]. Map: [link]. Co cau hoi gi cu nhan tin lai nhe!" Truoc 2 gio: "Con 2 gio nua su kien bat dau roi! Di som mot chut de tranh ket xe nhe :) Mong gap ban tai [dia diem]." Tac dong: Giam no-show 20–30%.

Use case 3 — On-the-day

Ho tro check-in va FAQ ngay su kien

Flow thuc te: Ngay su kien, khach nhan tin hoi "cho hoi ban hang am thuc o tang may?", "toilet o dau?", "lich trinh buoi chieu luc may gio?", "Wi-Fi password la gi?" → AI tra loi ngay lap tuc, giam tai dang ke cho staff. Voi su kien lon co nhieu phong/track: AI co the huong dan khach den dung phong theo nhu cau. Tac dong: Giam 50–60% cau hoi thu cong cho staff ngay su kien.

Use case 4 — Post-event

Thu thap feedback va follow-up sau event

Flow thuc te: 2 gio sau khi su kien ket thuc: "Cam on ban da tham du [ten su kien]! Ban thay su kien hom nay the nao? Danh gia giup minh nhe (1–5 sao): [link]" + "Tai lieu trinh bay cua cac dien gia o day: [link]" + "Su kien tiep theo cua chung minh la [ten su kien 2] vao [ngay]. Dang ky som de co uu tien: [link]." Tac dong: Ty le dien feedback tang tu 5–10% len 30–45% va tang chuyen doi sang su kien tiep theo.

Gioi han can biet: AI agent khong thay the duoc event manager

⚠️ Nhung gi AI agent KHONG lam duoc cho to chuc su kien:
• Xu ly su co thuc te ngay su kien: Dien mat, may mic hong, dien gia tre — can nguoi ra quyet dinh tuc thi, khong the doi AI phan tich.
• Dam phan hop dong voi doi tac: Venue, catering, equipment vendor — can event manager.
• Cham soc VIP khach moi: Sponsor, dien gia, khach dac biet — can cam ung cua nguoi that, khong phai bot.
• Ra quyet dinh khi co khung hoang: Khach hon nhan, tranh chap, su co an ninh — AI khong co tham quyen va ngu canh de xu ly.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

AI agent co xu ly duoc dang ky su kien co gia ve nhieu muc gia khac nhau khong?
Co — AI agent co the duoc cau hinh hieu cac loai ve khac nhau (VIP, standard, group), gia tuong ung, so luong con lai, va cac dieu kien dac biet (early bird, ma giam gia). Khach hoi ve loai nao, AI tra loi dung ve do, xu ly thanh toan va gui ve dien tu tu dong.
Lam the nao de AI agent xu ly khi nguoi dang ky muon chinh sua thong tin hoac huy ve?
AI agent co the xu ly cac thay doi thong tin don gian (ten, email, so dien thoai, so luong ve). Voi huy ve, AI kiem tra chinh sach hoan tien cua su kien roi thong bao cho nguoi dang ky, sau do chuyen cho team xu ly hoan tien neu can. Khong de AI tu dong hoan tien ma khong co nguoi duyet.
AI agent co the ho tro su kien online va offline cung luc khong?
Co — AI agent co the quan ly ca hai loai: su kien offline (dia chi, ban do, huong dan parking), su kien online (link Zoom/Meet, passcode, huong dan ky thuat), hoac su kien hybrid. Key la setup ro cac flow khac nhau cho moi loai tham du vien tu dau.
Voi su kien nhieu ngay, AI agent quan ly check-in tung ngay the nao?
AI agent co the cau hinh de gui nhac rieng cho moi ngay su kien: "Ngay mai la ngay 2 hoi nghi, nho co mat tai dia diem luc 8h30. Chuong trinh ngay 2: [link]." QR code check-in rieng cho moi ngay co the duoc tao tu dong va gui truoc 24 gio.
Chi phi setup AI agent cho cong ty su kien la bao nhieu?
Voi OpenClaw: 4–10 trieu dong setup mot lan (tuy do phuc tap cua quy trinh dang ky va so luong loai ve) + 500k–800k/thang van hanh. Cho su kien co tren 500 nguoi tham du, ROI hoan von trong 1–2 su kien nho giam tai team CSKH va tang ty le dien ve tu nhac tu dong.
