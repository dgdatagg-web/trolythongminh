OpenClaw vs Make.com — Cai Nao Phu Hop SME Viet Nam?

NOVA Team • 14/03/2026 • 8 phut doc

Tom Tat Nhanh

Make.com (Integromat) la cong cu automation ket noi 1500+ app theo visual workflow. OpenClaw la AI agent thong minh hoat dong qua Telegram/Zalo. Hai tool serve 2 nhu cau khac nhau: Make cho "ket noi app va tu dong hoa", OpenClaw cho "AI tra loi va xu ly yeu cau khach hang". SME Viet Nam khong co IT: OpenClaw re va de hon nhieu.

3 Diem Chinh Can Nho

  • Make.com = visual automation (scenario builder) ket noi 1500+ app theo rule
  • OpenClaw = AI agent hieu tieng Viet, tra loi 24/7 qua Zalo/Telegram, done-for-you
  • Khong co IT noi bo: OpenClaw an toan hon (Make can hieu API/webhook de van hanh tot)

Make.com La Gi?

Make.com (ten cu: Integromat) la nen tang automation no-code/low-code cho phep ket noi cac ung dung va xay dung workflow tu dong bang giao dien visual keo-tha. Manh hon Zapier ve kha nang xu ly data phuc tap, re hon Zapier, nhung cung kho hoc hon.

Make co ~1500 app integrations va module HTTP/Webhook cho phep ket noi bat ky API nao. Ly tuong cho developer va team co nen tang technical can automation backend phuc tap.

Luu y quan trong: Make.com tinh phi theo "operations" — moi buoc trong scenario la 1 operation. 1 automation phuc tap co the dung 10-20 ops moi lan chay. Voi 50 khach/ngay × 10 ops/khach = 15,000 ops/thang — vuot goi $10.59 trong ngay 20. OpenClaw phi co dinh theo thang, khong tinh theo so luong tuong tac.

Use Cases: Chon Cai Nao?

Dung OpenClaw khi:

Ban can AI tra loi khach hang 24/7 qua Zalo/Telegram, xu ly nhieu loai cau hoi, dat lich, tu van san pham, nho lich su khach hang. Khong co IT, muon co team VN ho tro, va muon phi on dinh co dinh moi thang.

Dung Make.com khi:

Ban can ket noi nhieu app voi nhau theo rule (Google Sheets → Mailchimp, Typeform → Notion → Slack). Co nguoi hieu ky thuat trong team, workflow co dinh va lap lai, va khong can AI hieu ngon ngu tu nhien.

Dung ca hai khi:

Make.com xu ly automation backend (dong bo don hang tu Shopify sang kho, gui email tu dong khi co don moi). OpenClaw xu ly front-end giao tiep voi khach (tra loi hoi hang, tu van, dat lich). Nhieu doanh nghiep scale dung ca hai song song.

Case Study: Cong Ty Van Tai

Cong ty van tai 20 xe o Binh Duong ban dau thu Make.com de quan ly booking. Team mat 3 tuan de setup scenario phuc tap, nhung van gap loi khi khach dat lich qua tin nhan Zalo (Zalo module Make khong day du).

Ho chuyen sang OpenClaw Pro (5 trieu/thang) cho kenh Zalo: AI tra loi lich xe, tinh gia cuoc, xac nhan booking — 24/7. Make.com van duoc giu de dong bo booking vao Google Sheet va gui bao cao cho owner. Hai tool phan cong ro rang, khong chong chep.

Ket qua: Tang 40% booking Zalo trong 30 ngay dau, giam 60% thoi gian xu ly thu cong cua nhan vien.

★ Ket Luan: OpenClaw Thang ro cho SME VN Khong Co IT

Make.com la cong cu manh cho developer va team technical. Nhung voi SME Viet Nam khong co IT noi bo, can giao tiep khach hang qua Zalo, va muon co ho tro tieng Viet — OpenClaw ro rang phu hop hon. Phi on dinh VND, done-for-you trong 48h, va AI that su hieu tieng Viet: day la nhung gi Make.com khong the cung cap cho thi truong Viet Nam.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

Make.com khac gi Zapier va n8n?

Make.com re hon Zapier, manh hon Zapier ve logic phuc tap (co filter, router, iterator). n8n la open-source self-hosted. Ca 3 la automation tools. Tat ca khac voi OpenClaw la AI agent.

OpenClaw co thay the Make.com duoc khong?

Khong hoan toan. Make.com gioi ve automation app-to-app. OpenClaw gioi ve AI conversation voi khach hang. Nhieu doanh nghiep dung ca hai cho 2 muc dich khac nhau.

Make.com co ho tro Zalo tot khong?

Han che. Module Zalo cua Make chua day du va khong duoc update thuong xuyen. OpenClaw ho tro Zalo native va duoc xay dung cho thi truong VN.

SME khong co IT nen chon cai nao?

OpenClaw. Make.com can hieu API, webhook va data mapping de van hanh hieu qua. OpenClaw done-for-you: team setup va ho tro toan bo bang tieng Viet.

Chi phi Make.com tinh nhu the nao?

Make tinh phi theo "operations" — moi buoc trong workflow la 1 op. Workflow phuc tap co the dung 10-20 ops/lan chay. Chi phi tang theo so luong tuong tac. OpenClaw phi co dinh khong tinh theo so luong.
