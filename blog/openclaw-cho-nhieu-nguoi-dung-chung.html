OpenClaw Cho Nhieu Nguoi Dung Chung: Setup Team Va Business

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 7 phut doc
💬 Tra Loi Nhanh

OpenClaw ho tro nhieu nguoi dung thong qua he thong allowlist — them so dien thoai hoac Telegram ID cua tung thanh vien vao file config. Mot instance co the phuc vu nhieu nguoi dong thoi, voi kha nang cau hinh quyen truy cap rieng cho tung kenh. Kho nhat khong phai la "co lam duoc khong" ma la "cau hinh nhu the nao dung cach" — vi phai can bang giua bao mat (khong de nguoi la truy cap), tinh nang (tung nhom co quyen gi), va hieu nang (VPS co xu ly duoc khong). Day la ly do nhieu founders co bot ca nhan roi bi mac ket khi muon mo rong cho ca team.

📋 Diem Chinh Can Nho

  • OpenClaw dung duoc cho nhieu nguoi — nhung can cau hinh allowlist va permissions ro rang
  • Tung kenh Telegram co the co quyen khac nhau — nhom noi bo khac nhom khach hang
  • VPS phai du manh de xu ly concurrent requests khi nhieu nguoi dung cung luc
  • Them/xoa nguoi dung can restart gateway — khong co admin panel GUI
  • Goi 2M/thang cua NOVA bao gom quan ly danh sach nguoi dung va phan quyen lien tuc

Tai sao chuyen tu bot ca nhan sang bot team lai kho?

Khi anh/chi lan dau dung OpenClaw — thong thuong chi co 1 nguoi (chinh anh/chi) nhan tin voi bot. Moi thu don gian: 1 Telegram account, 1 chat window, 1 nguoi.

Nhung khi muon cho ca team cung dung — moi su viec phuc tap len nhanh:

  • Bot can biet ai duoc phep nhan tin — va ai khong
  • Nhom noi bo (nhan vien) va nhom khach hang co the can nguong quyen khac nhau
  • Nhieu nguoi nhan tin cung luc → VPS phai xu ly duoc concurrent requests
  • Khi co nhan vien moi vao hoac nghi viec → can cap nhat danh sach theo thoi gian thuc

Nhieu founders da nhan ra dieu nay: bot ca nhan chay tot, nhung khong biet bat dau tu dau khi muon share cho team.

He thong allowlist hoat dong nhu the nao

Allowlist la co che bao mat chinh cua OpenClaw — no quy dinh ai duoc phep tuong tac voi bot.

Trong file cau hinh openclaw.json, co truong allowFrom — list cac so dien thoai hoac Telegram ID duoc phep nhan tin:

  • Neu so/ID co trong danh sach → bot tra loi binh thuong
  • Neu khong co trong danh sach → bot im lang, khong nhan tin, ghi log "blocked"
  • Neu de danh sach trong → bot chi nhan tin tu nguoi da duoc pair (mode ca nhan)

De them thanh vien moi:

  1. SSH vao VPS cua anh/chi
  2. Mo file ~/.openclaw/openclaw.json
  3. Them so dien thoai hoac Telegram ID vao mang allowFrom
  4. Restart gateway: openclaw gateway restart
  5. Thanh vien moi co the nhan tin voi bot ngay

⚠️ Luu y bao mat: Khong de allowFrom rong neu deploy cong khai — bat ky ai biet username bot deu co the nhan tin. Luon co danh sach ro rang. Voi nhom khach hang — can kiem soat can than hon la voi nhom noi bo.

Cau hinh quyen theo tung kenh Telegram

Mot trong nhung tinh nang manh nhat cua OpenClaw la co the cau hinh quyen khac nhau cho tung kenh:

Kenh / Nhom Cau hinh phu hop Vi du su dung
DM ca nhan (chu bot) Full access, khong gioi han Anh/chi tro chuyen truc tiep, day du tinh nang
Nhom noi bo (team) Allowlist nhan vien, requireMention: true Nhan vien hoi bot khi can, bot chi reply khi duoc tag
Nhom khach hang Allowlist gioi han, Skills/SOUL chi pham vi CSKH Bot CSKH tra loi cau hoi san pham, bao gio giao hang
Nhom cong khai Rat han che, chi FAQ co dinh Thong bao tu dong, tra loi FAQ khong can context

Pattern pho bien nhat cho SME VN: 3 kenh rieng biet — DM chu, nhom nhan vien noi bo (5–15 nguoi), va nhom khach hang (moi khach hang 1 group rieng hoac chung 1 nhom CSKH).

VPS can du manh de phuc vu nhieu nguoi

Day la van de thuong bi bo qua: khi chi 1 nguoi dung, VPS 2GB RAM du o. Khi 10 nguoi nhan tin dong thoi — chuyen khac.

So nguoi dung dong thoi VPS khuyen nghi Chi phi uoc tinh/thang
1–3 nguoi 2GB RAM, 1 vCPU ~240,000–300,000d (Vultr/DO)
5–10 nguoi 4GB RAM, 2 vCPU ~480,000–600,000d
15–30 nguoi 8GB RAM, 4 vCPU ~960,000–1,200,000d
30+ nguoi / khach hang Can xem xet kien truc rieng Lien he de tu van cu the

Ngoai VPS, con phai xem xet API rate limits cua provider. Anthropic Claude Sonnet co gioi han request dong thoi (concurrent) — neu nhieu nguoi nhan tin cung luc va moi request deu can AI response, co the bi 429 rate limit neuu khong cau hinh queue dung.

Quan ly nguoi dung theo thoi gian — viec khong co diem ket

Mot van de thuc te anh/chi se gap sau khi setup xong: quan ly danh sach nguoi dung theo thoi gian.

  • Nhan vien moi vao → can them vao allowlist
  • Nhan vien nghi viec → can xoa khoi danh sach (quan trong ve bao mat)
  • Khach hang moi → co them vao nhom CSKH khong?
  • Thay doi VPS hoac cau hinh → phai restart, kiem tra allowlist con dung khong

Voi bot ca nhan — viec nay don gian vi it nguoi. Voi bot team 20–50 nguoi — day tro thanh cong viec quan ly deu dan, can co nguoi chiu trach nhiem.

Day la 1 trong nhung ly do goi Basic 2M/thang co y nghia voi mo hinh team: khong phai chi de setup 1 lan, ma de co nguoi quan ly lien tuc — them nguoi, xoa nguoi, dieu chinh quyen, xu ly khi co van de.

Sau khi setup multi-user — cac buoc toi uu tiep theo

Khi da co nhieu nguoi dung, cac nhu cau toi uu cung tang len. Hai viec pho bien nhat:

1. Toi uu SOUL.md theo tung nhom: Bot noi chhuyen voi nhan vien noi bo se khac voi bot cham soc khach hang. Can chinh SOUL.md phu hop — hoac setup 2 config rieng cho 2 channel. Xem bai Toi uu OpenClaw sau khi setup de hieu cach lam dieu nay.

2. Ket noi du lieu thuc cua business: Khi co nhieu nguoi dung — gia tri bot tang rat nhieu neu no ket noi duoc voi du lieu thuc: Sheets bao cao, Gmail, Calendar chung cua team. Xem bai Ket noi OpenClaw voi Google Workspace de biet cach lam.

Neu chua co OpenClaw chay on dinh tren VPS, nen doc bai OpenClaw cai dat nhu the nao truoc de co foundation vung chac truoc khi mo rong cho team.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

OpenClaw co ho tro nhieu nguoi dung cung luc khong?
Co. OpenClaw ho tro nhieu nguoi dung thong qua he thong allowlist — them so dien thoai hoac Telegram ID cua tung thanh vien vao file config. Mot instance co the phuc vu nhieu nguoi dong thoi, voi kha nang cau hinh quyen truy cap rieng cho tung kenh Telegram.
Lam the nao de them thanh vien moi vao bot OpenClaw cua team?
Can them so dien thoai hoac Telegram ID cua ho vao truong allowFrom trong file config openclaw.json, sau do restart gateway. Viec nay can truy cap vao server va chinh sua file config — khong phai qua Telegram hay giao dien do hoa. Moi thay doi can restart service de ap dung.
Co the cau hinh phan quyen khac nhau cho tung nguoi dung khong?
Co the cau hinh quyen khac nhau cho tung kenh Telegram — vi du nhom noi bo co day du quyen, nhom khach hang chi tra loi trong pham vi han che. Cau hinh theo channel la cach pho bien nhat de phan biet nhom nhan vien va nhom khach hang.
Mot OpenClaw instance co the phuc vu bao nhieu nguoi dong thoi?
Phu thuoc vao VPS spec va API provider. VPS 2GB RAM + Sonnet model xu ly duoc 5–15 request dong thoi. Neu co nhieu hon 20 nguoi dung hoat dong thuong xuyen, can nang cap VPS len 4–8GB RAM hoac toi uu cau hinh concurrency.
Co dich vu nao setup OpenClaw multi-user cho toan team khong?
Co. NOVA cung cap dich vu setup OpenClaw multi-user — cau hinh allowlist, phan quyen theo kenh, toi uu cho nhieu nguoi dung dong thoi. Goi 2M/thang bao gom ca viec quan ly va cap nhat danh sach nguoi dung sau do. Dat lich tu van mien phi de bat dau.
