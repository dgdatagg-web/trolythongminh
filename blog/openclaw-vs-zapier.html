OpenClaw vs Zapier — So Sanh Chi Phi va Kha Nang cho Doanh Nghiep Nho

NOVA Team • 14/03/2026 • 8 phut doc

Tom Tat Nhanh

Zapier gioi ve ket noi app theo rule co dinh (automation). OpenClaw la AI agent thong minh hieu tieng Viet, nho context, xu ly yeu cau phuc tap. Voi SME Viet Nam: OpenClaw re hon 60% va phu hop hon cho cong viec hang ngay.

3 Diem Chinh Can Nho

  • Zapier = automation (neu X thi Y). OpenClaw = AI agent (hieu ngon ngu tu nhien, suy luan)
  • Zapier Professional tu $49/thang USD — OpenClaw Pro chi 5 trieu VND/thang
  • SME Viet Nam can AI tra loi khach 24/7 tieng Viet: OpenClaw thang clear

Hai Tool Nay La Gi?

Khi tim hieu ve tu dong hoa cong viec, ban se gap ca Zapier va OpenClaw. Nhung tren thuc te, day la 2 loai cong cu hoan toan khac nhau — giai quyet 2 van de khac nhau.

Zapier la nen tang automation ket noi 6.000+ ung dung. Khi su kien X xay ra (ai do dien form, gui email), Zapier tu dong chay hanh dong Y (tao row trong Sheet, gui Slack). Don gian, manh, nhung theo quy tac co dinh.

OpenClaw la AI agent gateway — chuyen bien AI (Claude, GPT) thanh nhan vien ao thong minh hoat dong qua Telegram, Zalo, WhatsApp. Thay vi theo rule co dinh, AI co the hieu nguoi dung muon gi, nho context cuoc tro chuyen, va xu ly cac yeu cau khong lap di lap lai.

  • AI hieu tieng Viet, giao tiep tu nhien
  • Done-for-you — team setup cho ban
  • Gia VND, khong lo ty gia USD
  • Support Zalo — kenh pho bien nhat VN
  • Custom theo nganh: spa, gym, BDS, nha hang
  • Nho context, khong phai tra loi di tra loi lai

✗ Han Che

  • Can thoi gian onboarding 48h ban dau
  • Khong phu hop neu chi can sync data giua cac app

Zapier

✓ Diem Manh

  • Giao dien drag-and-drop de hieu

✗ Han Che

  • Khong co AI reasoning — chi theo rule co dinh
  • Chi phi leo thang nhanh theo so tasks (Zaps)
  • Khong hieu tieng Viet, interface tieng Anh
  • Khong ho tro Zalo
  • Khi can logic phuc tap, nguoi dung thuong bi stuck
  • Khong co "done-for-you" — phai tu lam hoac tra them

So Sanh Chi Phi Thuc Te

Day la phan nhieu nguoi quan tam nhat khi chon cong cu cho doanh nghiep nho.

Goi OpenClaw (VND/thang) Zapier (USD/thang) Zapier (VND tuong duong)
Co ban 2.000.000 $29 (han che 750 tasks) ~720.000 VND
Trung cap 5.000.000 $73 (2000 tasks) ~1.800.000 VND
Nang cao 25.000.000/nam $199+ (50k+ tasks) ~5.000.000 VND

Luu y quan trong: Gia Zapier tinh theo so tasks (Zap runs). 1 khach hang hoi 5 cau = 5 tasks. Voi business co 50 khach/ngay, 2000 tasks/thang het trong 13 ngay. OpenClaw khong tinh phi theo so lan hoi.

Khi Nao Dung OpenClaw, Khi Nao Dung Zapier?

Chon OpenClaw khi:

  • Ban can AI tra loi khach hang 24/7 qua Telegram/Zalo/WhatsApp
  • Khach hoi nhieu loai cau hoi khac nhau, khong theo script co dinh
  • Ban muon AI nho ten khach, lich su mua hang, so thich
  • Nganh co nhieu tu chuyen mon tieng Viet (bat dong san, y te, luat)
  • Khong co IT noi bo, can team ho tro setup va van hanh
  • Thanh toan VND, khong muon lo ty gia

Chon Zapier khi:

  • Ban can ket noi cac app voi nhau (Gmail → Sheets, Typeform → Slack)
  • Workflow co rule ro rang, lap di lap lai, khong can AI suy luan
  • Team da quen dung cac app My va co nguoi quan ly technical
  • Khong can ho tro tieng Viet hay Zalo

Canh bao: Nhieu SME mua Zapier vi "nghe ten", sau do phat hien khong dung den phan lon tinh nang va chi phi tang qua nhanh. Hay xac dinh ro ban can "ket noi app" hay "AI tra loi khach" truoc khi quyet dinh.

Use Case Thuc Te: SME Viet Nam

Truong hop 1: Spa / Salon

Chi Lan co spa tai TP.HCM, nhan 30-50 tin nhan/ngay hoi lich dat, gia dich vu, khuyen mai. Truoc day mat 2 tieng moi ngay de tra loi thu cong. Voi OpenClaw Pro (5 trieu/thang), AI tra loi 24/7, dat lich tu dong, nhac lich cho khach qua Zalo. Chi tiet kiem duoc 50+ tieng/thang.

Truong hop 2: Agency Marketing

Anh Nam co agency, can dong bo lead tu Typeform sang CRM, gui email chao mung tu dong, update Slack khi co deal moi. Day la bai toan Zapier giai quyet tot. Khong can AI — chi can ket noi app theo rule co dinh. Anh dang dung Zapier Starter $29/thang va hai long.

Truong hop 3: Trung tam Anh ngu

Truong anh ngu can ca hai: AI tra loi phu huynh hoi ve hoc phi, lich hoc qua Zalo (OpenClaw), dong thoi dong bo danh sach hoc vien tu Google Form sang HRM (Zapier). Ho dung ca hai tool theo muc dich khac nhau — va thay hop ly.

★ Ket Luan: OpenClaw Thang ro rang cho SME Viet Nam can AI

Neu muc tieu chinh cua ban la tra loi khach hang thong minh 24/7, tieng Viet, qua Zalo/Telegram — OpenClaw la lua chon ro rang hon. Chi phi co dinh bang VND, co team ho tro, khong lo Zap chay het. Zapier tot hon khi ban can ket noi nhieu app My voi nhau va khong can AI reasoning.

Cau Hoi Thuong Gap

OpenClaw co thay the duoc Zapier khong?

Khong hoan toan. Zapier gioi ve ket noi app theo rule co dinh. OpenClaw la AI agent thong minh hieu ngon ngu tu nhien. Hai tool phuc vu 2 nhu cau khac nhau.

Zapier co dat hon OpenClaw khong?

Phu thuoc cach dung. Zapier Professional $49 USD (~1.2 trieu VND) nhung tang nhanh theo tasks. OpenClaw Pro 5 trieu VND co dinh, khong tinh phi theo so tin nhan.

SME Viet Nam nen chon cai nao?

Can AI tra loi khach 24/7 tieng Viet qua Zalo/Telegram: chon OpenClaw. Can ket noi Gmail, Shopify, Slack theo rule co dinh: chon Zapier. Nhieu business can ca hai.

Co the dung ca hai cung nhau khong?

Co. Zapier xu ly workflow automation, OpenClaw xu ly AI conversation. Nhieu doanh nghiep dung song song theo muc dich rieng biet.

Can biet lap trinh de dung OpenClaw khong?

Khong. Team trolythongminh.io.vn setup toan bo cho ban trong 48h. Ban chi can su dung qua Telegram hoac Zalo nhu binh thuong.
