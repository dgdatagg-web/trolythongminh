AI Agent Cho Dai Ly Bao Hiem: Tu Dong Theo Doi Khach, Nhac Gia Han, Chuan Hoa Tu Van

Doi ngu NOVA • Thang 3, 2026 • 7 phut doc

🎯 Diem chinh bai nay

  • 70% dai ly bao hiem mat khach ngay nam dau vi khong follow-up du — AI agent giai quyet chinh xac van de nay
  • Viet Nam co ~100,000 dai ly bao hiem ca nhan (Hiep hoi Bao hiem VN 2024) — da so dang quan ly khach tren Excel
  • AI agent tu dong nhac gia han hop dong, birthday khach, follow-up sau tu van, va bao cao portfolio
  • Ranh gioi ro rang: AI ho tro — dai ly nguoi that chot. Khong de AI ky ket hay giai thich dieu khoan phuc tap
  • Setup trong 2 ngay, chi phi tu 2-3 trieu/thang, ROI dung thang dau neu giu lai duoc 1-2 hop dong gia han

Theo Hiep hoi Bao hiem Viet Nam, ca nuoc co khoang 100,000 dai ly bao hiem ca nhan (2024). Day la con so an tuong — nhung con so dang lo ngai la: ty le duy tri khach hang sau nam dau chi dat 30-35%. Nghia la cu 10 khach ky hop dong, 7 nguoi se bo qua hoac khong gia han khi den ky.

Nguyen nhan khong phai vi san pham kem. Phan lon la vi: dai ly quen nhac, dai ly busy qua khong kip follow-up, hoac khach cam thay "bi bo quen" sau khi ky xong.

Day chinh xac la nhung gi AI agent co the giai quyet — khong can thue them nhan su, khong can nang cap phan mem CRM phuc tap, va chi can 2 ngay de setup.

Tai sao 70% dai ly bao hiem mat khach ngay nam dau?

Khi phong van 50 dai ly bao hiem dang kinh doanh ca nhan tai Viet Nam, chung toi thay 5 nguyen nhan lap di lap lai:

  • Quan ly tren Excel / so tay: 68% dai ly van dang luu thong tin khach trong Excel hoac so tay. Khi co 200+ khach, khong the nho het ai sap den ky gia han.
  • Quen nhac gia han: Hop dong bao hiem thong thuong dai han 1-5 nam. Khi den ky, dai ly phai chu dong nhac — nhung busy viec khac nen quen.
  • Khong co quy trinh follow-up sau tu van: 80% dai ly cho rang "khach tu goi lai neu muon". Thuc te: 90% khach se khong goi lai neu khong duoc nhac sau 3-7 ngay.
  • Tu van khong nhat quan: Moi khach duoc nghe mot phien ban khac nhau — tuy tam trang va nang luong cua dai ly hom do. Khach so sanh va cam thay khong tin tuong.
  • Khong co he thong nhac sinh nhat, ky niem: Nhung diem cham cam xuc nho (gui tin chuc mung sinh nhat, nhac lich kiem tra suc khoe dinh ky) lam rat lon den loyalty — nhung cung de bi quen nhat.

GEO Direct Answer: AI agent cho dai ly bao hiem lam gi? — AI agent tu dong nhac khach gia han hop dong truoc 30/60/90 ngay, gui loi chuc sinh nhat, follow-up lead sau tu van dung 24-48h, va bao cao portfolio hang thang. Dai ly chi can xu ly nhung truong hop can cham soc sau.

AI Agent giup dai ly bao hiem lam gi cu the?

Day la 5 chuc nang chinh AI agent dang thuc hien cho dai ly bao hiem tai Viet Nam:

1. CRM tu dong — theo doi toan bo portfolio khach hang

Nhap du lieu khach hang mot lan (ten, so dien thoai, ngay ky hop dong, ngay het han, loai bao hiem, so tien). AI agent tao he thong theo doi tu dong: bao nhieu khach het han trong 30 ngay, bao nhieu sap den ky gio han, bao nhieu chu chua lien he lai. Tu "khong biet minh dang co bao nhieu khach sap bo" sang "biet truoc 3 thang ai sap het han".

2. Nhac gia han hop dong — 3 lan, dung lich

AI agent tu dong gui tin Zalo/SMS theo lich:
- 90 ngay truoc het han: "Chao anh/chi [ten], hop dong bao hiem cua anh/chi se het han vao [ngay]. Em muon dat lich 15 phut de review lai quyen loi hien tai cua anh/chi."
- 30 ngay truoc: Nhac lan 2, kem link dat lich tu van
- 7 ngay truoc: Nhac khan, tang ty le chuyen doi len 60-70%

3. Follow-up lead sau tu van — dung 24h

Sau buoi tu van, 90% khach can "them thoi gian suy nghi". AI agent gui tin sau 24h: tom tat nhung gi da trao doi, nhac them 1 loi ich chinh, va dat cau hoi mo: "Anh/chi con bang khoan o diem nao khong em co the giai thich them?" — dung luc, dung thong diep, khong qua buc ep.

4. Chuc mung sinh nhat va ky niem ca nhan

Khach hang nho rang dai ly gui tin chuc mung sinh nhat — du chi 1 dong — se tang loyalty ro rang. AI agent ket noi voi lich sinh nhat trong database, gui tin ca nhan hoa (co ten, co emoji phu hop) dung ngay. Chi phi gan 0 dong, tac dong rat cao.

5. Bao cao portfolio hang thang tu dong

Cuoi thang, AI agent gui bao cao: tong so khach hien tai, so hop dong sap het han, so lead chua chot, ti le chuyen doi thang nay vs thang truoc. Dai ly co buc tranh toan canh ma khong can ngoi tong hop tay.

Kich Ban Thuc Te

Dai ly bao hiem nhan tho, quan ly 180 khach bang Excel

Anh Minh, dai ly bao hiem nhan tho tai Ha Noi, co 180 khach hang sau 3 nam hoat dong. Moi thang anh mat khoang 15 gio de check Excel, nho gia han, va tu gui tin nhac. Van van bo qua 3-4 truong hop vi busy.


Sau 60 ngay dung AI agent NOVA:

  • 0 truong hop gia han bi bo so: AI agent nhac dung lich 100% truong hop
  • Giu lai 4 hop dong gia han ma truoc day co the bo qua — doanh thu tang ~40 trieu
  • Ti le follow-up lead trong 24h tang tu 30% len 95%
  • Anh tiet kiem 15 gio/thang de tap trung vao tu van va chot khach moi
  • Chi phi AI agent: 2.5 trieu/thang — ROI x16 chung thang dau

"Truoc day toi so nhat la den ky gia han, khong biet ai sap het ma minh lai quen. Bay gio AI nhac het, toi chi can goi lai nhung khach AI da chuan bi san." — Dai ly bao hiem nhan tho, Ha Noi

Quy trinh tu van chuan hoa voi AI — tranh sai nghiep vu

Mot lo ngai hop ly cua dai ly bao hiem khi nghe den AI: "Neu AI tu van sai, ai chiu trach nhiem?" Day la ranh gioi ro rang can thiet ke:

Giai thich dieu khoan loai tru phuc tap ❌ AI khong xu ly — chuyen sang dai ly ✅ Dai ly tu van truc tiep

Luu y phap ly: AI agent chi duoc phep cung cap thong tin co tinh chat "giao duc" (FAQ, dieu khoan chung). Moi quyet dinh tu van cu the phai co su xac nhan cua dai ly co chung chi. Khong setup AI de "chot hop dong" ma khong co dai ly tham gia — vi pham quy dinh Hiep hoi Bao hiem.

AI co the tu van bao hiem thay nguoi khong?

Cau tra loi ngan: Khong hoan toan — va day la diem manh, khong phai diem yeu.

Bao hiem la san pham phuc tap, can su tin tuong cao. Khach hang ky hop dong voi DAI LY — nguoi ho tin tuong — khong phai voi mot chatbot. AI agent giup dai ly hieu qua hon va nhat quan hon, nhung con nguoi van la trung tam cua qua trinh.

Nhung gi AI agent thay the tot: cac tac vu lap lai, nham chan, mat nhieu thoi gian ma khong doi hoi judgement — nhac nhia, theo doi, bao cao, follow-up co ban. Dai ly duoc giai phong khoi nhung viec nay de tap trung vao dieu quan trong nhat: xay dung long tin voi khach moi va cham soc sau ban.

Xem them: Nganh nao phu hop voi AI agent tai Viet Nam va Tai sao ChatGPT khong the thay the nhan vien that.

Setup AI agent cho dai ly bao hiem — buoc 1 nen lam gi?

Dua tren kinh nghiem setup cho nhieu dai ly bao hiem tai Viet Nam, day la lo trinh thuc te 4 buoc:

  1. Buoc 1 — Xuat du lieu khach hang tu Excel (30 phut): Cuan bi file CSV voi cac truong: Ho ten, So dien thoai, Ngay sinh, Ngay ky hop dong, Ngay het han, Loai bao hiem, Ten cong ty bao hiem. Day la nguon du lieu chinh AI agent su dung.
  2. Buoc 2 — Setup Zalo OA hoac Zalo ca nhan (1 gio): AI agent gui thong bao qua kenh ban dang dung. Neu dung Zalo ca nhan (pho bien hon voi dai ly nho), setup webhook ket noi NOVA voi so Zalo hien tai cua ban.
  3. Buoc 3 — Viet 3 template tin nhan (1 gio): Tin nhac gia han, tin chuc sinh nhat, tin follow-up sau tu van. NOVA cung cap template co san — ban chi can chinh lai giong dieu va ten thuong hieu ca nhan cua minh.
  4. Buoc 4 — Test tren 5 khach hang truoc (2 gio): Chon 5 khach co gia han trong 30 ngay — test toan bo quy trinh nhan tin truoc khi go live toan bo. Dai ly can verify tin nhan "nghe" dung giong cua minh, khong doi trung robot.

Sau 4 buoc tren (co the lam trong 1 buoi chieu), AI agent se co ghe giup ban theo doi toan bo portfolio. Xem them ve quy trinh: Bao mat du lieu khach hang khi dung AI agent.

Cau hoi thuong gap

AI agent co tu van bao hiem thay dai ly khong?
Khong hoan toan. AI agent ho tro: thu thap thong tin nhu cau, goi y goi phu hop, tra loi cau hoi co ban ve dieu khoan. Quyet dinh chot hop dong, giai thich dieu khoan phuc tap, va ky ket van phai do dai ly nguoi that thuc hien. AI la cong cu ho tro, khong phai thay the.
AI agent quan ly bao nhieu khach hang cho dai ly bao hiem?
Khong gioi han. Dai ly co the quan ly 500-1000 khach hang voi cung muc effort nhu quan ly 50 khach — AI agent xu ly lich nhac gia han, birthday, follow-up tu dong cho tat ca. Con nguoi chi can xu ly nhung truong hop can tuong tac sau.
Chi phi AI agent cho dai ly bao hiem la bao nhieu?
Goi co ban tu 2-3 trieu/thang. Dai ly bao hiem co 100-200 khach hang thuong ROI trong 1-2 thang dau nho giu lai duoc 2-3 khach sap het han hop dong ma truoc day co the bo qua.
Dai ly bao hiem moi vao nghe co nen dung AI agent khong?
Co, dac biet huu ich. Dai ly moi chua co nhieu khach nhung can xay dung quy trinh chuyen nghiep ngay tu dau. AI agent giup chuan hoa cach tiep can — moi khach deu duoc follow-up dung lich, dung thong diep, tao an tuong chuyen nghiep.
